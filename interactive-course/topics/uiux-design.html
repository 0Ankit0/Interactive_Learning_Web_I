Core Design Principles

Understanding these fundamental principles is essential for creating effective user interfaces:

Visual Hierarchy

Guide users' attention through strategic use of size, color, and positioning to create clear information flow.

Consistency

Maintain uniform design patterns, interactions, and terminology throughout your application.

Feedback

Provide clear, immediate responses to user actions to confirm interactions and system status.

Simplicity

Embrace minimalism and remove unnecessary elements to focus on what matters most to users.

Affordance

Design elements that clearly indicate their function and how they can be interacted with.

Accessibility

Design inclusive experiences that work for users with diverse abilities and needs.

UX Research Methods

Learn about essential UX research methods to understand your users better:

User Interviews

One-on-one conversations to understand user needs, motivations, and pain points.

When to use:

  • Early research phases
  • Understanding user motivations
  • Gathering qualitative insights

Best practices:

  • Prepare open-ended questions
  • Listen more than you speak
  • Record with permission

Surveys

Collect quantitative data from a large number of users about their preferences and behaviors.

When to use:

  • Gathering quantitative data
  • Validating assumptions
  • Large sample sizes

Best practices:

  • Keep surveys short
  • Use clear, unbiased questions
  • Mix question types

Usability Testing

Observe users interacting with your product to identify usability issues and areas for improvement.

When to use:

  • Testing prototypes
  • Validating design decisions
  • Finding usability issues

Best practices:

  • Define clear tasks
  • Encourage thinking aloud
  • Don't guide users

Personas

Create fictional characters based on real user data to represent different user types and their goals.

When to use:

  • Design decision making
  • Team alignment
  • Feature prioritization

Best practices:

  • Base on real data
  • Keep them simple
  • Update regularly
